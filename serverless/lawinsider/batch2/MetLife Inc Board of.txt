[[Organization 1 Name]] Proportionate Total Shareholder Return for 2010, as defined for [[Organization 1 Name]] in Section 1(d)(2) of the form of Management Performance Share Agreement used for awards for the 2010-2012 Performance Period (the “Performance Share Agreement”) with respect to 2010.
