The company generally pays non-employee directors a fee of $[[USD Value 1: Number]] for each Board meeting attended, $[[USD Value 2: Number]] for each committee meeting attended, and reimburses them for out-of-pocket expenses of attending meetings.

Pursuant to the non-employee director option program adopted on [[Date 2: Date]], which is similar to the program that was suspended in 2004, each non-employee director will also receive an automatic grant of an option to purchase 2,500 shares of common stock annually upon his re-election to the Board by the stockholders. These options were issued at a per share exercise price equal to the fair market value of one share of common stock on [[Date 1]], and expire ten years from the grant date.
